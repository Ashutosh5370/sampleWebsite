About Us

Our Mission is to provide organic and fresh mangoes and to reduce gap between farms to you. We would like to ensure the mangoes of best quality at your doorsteps. We are a team of farmers from Konkan belt like Ratnagiri, Devgad. Kelshi. Murud & Valsad. Yes, we are the growers of Authentic Devgad Hapus and Ratnagiri Hapus. Which is commonly known as Alphonso varieties of Mango
